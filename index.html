Research & Development

2010
2011

Krommy.

Snap Filters before Snapchat. 2011

After releasing Kromath, the first real-time Green Screen compositing application for iOS, Hassan furthered the research into computer vision and developed an early concept of an entertainment app resembling Snapchat filter yet almost a decade ahead.


2013

Onelapse.

Live Photos Before Apple. 2013

Following Ultrakam’s success, Hassan envisioned a new type of media object that would blend the high-resolution video with the experience of taking photos. “Taking photos from videos” as He described it, would allow the user to choose that perfect moment just after capturing the event. This concept was 2 years later productized by Apple as “Live Photos” and released with the iPhone 6s in 2015


Cinekolor.

High-End Color Correction Before Adobe. 2014

Hassan developed Cinekolor after being challenged by their VFX peers to create an application that would be able to apply professional 3-stage color correction and then export the style (3D LUT) in a format compatible with High-End film production software. The application was launched and featured all over the world in 2014.


2014

Renderfarm X.

Patent Pending Video Rendering Platform. 2018

When faced with the challenge of delegating the slow rendering process of video effects from the iPhone application into the FlyrTV servers, Hassan envisioned a revolutionary hardware and software architecture that allowed to reuse the same rendering engine powering the iOS application but provide a scalable backend-side rendering solution. The company was acquired in 2018.
