## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Visual Style
- **Reference Sites:**
  - https://www.vatsalbhardwaj.com/ (structure)
  - https://www.wakenai.com/ (style)
  - Apple.com aesthetic (clean, bold, impactful)

### Key Design Principles
1. **NO glassmorphism** - was requested initially, then rejected
2. **Clean and professional** like Apple.com
3. **FULL-WIDTH VIDEOS** - all videos must be large, prominent, 16:9 aspect ratio
4. **Museum exhibition format** - wide breathing spaces between sections
5. **NO EMOJIS** anywhere
6. **Bold typography** - Google Fonts (Playfair Display, Montserrat, Inter)
7. **Pure black background** (#000000)
8. **Apple blue accents** (#0071E3)
9. **Generous spacing** - lots of white space
10. **Professional headings** - clear, large, readable
